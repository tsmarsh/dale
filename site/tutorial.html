Dale Tutorial

Set up your Telegram subscription community in minutes.

Home → Tutorial

1. Onboarding

The onboarding wizard walks you through connecting your services in three steps.

Step 1

Display Name

Enter the name your subscribers will see.

Step 2

Telegram Bot Token

Paste the bot token from @BotFather on Telegram.

Step 3

Stripe Credentials

Enter your Stripe secret key and webhook signing secret.

Done

Success

You'll see your tenant ID and the Stripe webhook URL to add to your Stripe dashboard.

2. Dashboard

After onboarding, you land on your dashboard with a welcome message and room overview.

3. Room Management

Rooms are the subscription units your users pay to access. Each room maps to a Telegram group.

Empty State

When you have no rooms yet, you'll see a prompt to create one.

Create a Room

Click "Create Room" to open the form. Enter a name and Stripe payment link.

Room List

Your rooms appear as cards with key details at a glance.

Room Detail

Click a room to see its full details, payment link, and subscriber list.

Edit Room

Click "Edit" to update room settings inline.

4. Settings

View and update your profile, and re-register your Telegram webhook.
